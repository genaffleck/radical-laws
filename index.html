LAWS OF RADICALS

Let \(n\), \(m\) be positive integers greater than \(1\), \(a\) and \(b\) be positive real numbers

Rule 1: \((\sqrt[n]{a})^n\)

Rule 2: \( \sqrt[n]{a\cdot b}=\sqrt[n]{a} \cdot \sqrt[n]{b} \)

Rule 3: \( \sqrt[n]{\dfrac{a}{b}}=\dfrac{\sqrt[n]{a}}{\sqrt[n]{b}} \)

Rule 4: \( \sqrt[m]{\sqrt[n]{a}}=\sqrt[mn]{a} \)

by: Sir Ruiz
